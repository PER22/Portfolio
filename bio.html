Hi, I'm Pat Reilly!

I'm a software enthusiast and currently a bootcamper at General Assembly, pursuing my passion for software engineering. I hold a Computer Science degree from the University of California, Riverside, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving.

Beyond coding, I have diverse interests and hobbies. I love building and flying FPV drones, hiking, practicing yoga, and playing the guitar and piano. Recently, I've been dabbling in FDM 3D printing and CAD.

I live in Oakland with my lovely girlfiend, my loving 8 year old Husky/Golden Retriever mix, and a mischievious black cat.

I value honesty, friendship, and respect. I believe in giving my best effort to everything I do and I am committed to constantly learning and growing.

I am currently enrolled in the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive program, set to complete it in June 2023. My goal is to land an entry-level position in software engineering upon graduation. With my technical skills, passion for coding, and commitment to personal and professional growth, I hope to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.
