Hi, I'm Pat Reilly!

I'm a software enthusiast and currently a bootcamper at General Assembly, pursuing my passion for software engineering. I hold a Computer Science degree from the University of California, Riverside, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving.

Beyond coding, I love building and flying FPV drones, hiking, practicing yoga, and playing the guitar and piano. Recently, I've been dabbling in FDM 3D printing and CAD.

I believe in giving my best effort to everything I do. I am committed to constantly learning and growing, and always have small hobby projects, coding-related or otherwise, going on the side to expand my horizons. I am eager to work in a technical role for many reasons, but especially to learn from more experienced coworkers and to experience working on a larger codebase.

Currently enrolled in the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive program, set to complete it in June 2023. My goal is to land an entry-level position in software engineering upon graduation. With my technical skills, passion for coding, and commitment to personal and professional growth, I hope to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry. Connect with me via the Contact page, if you are interested in discussing any projects, roles, or ideas.

On a more personal note, I live in Oakland with my lovely girlfiend, my gentle old Husky/Golden Retriever mix, and a troublesome black cat.
