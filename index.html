Hey! Thanks for checking out my site!

My name is Padraic Reilly, or Pat to my friends. I recently graduated the Bournes College of Engineering at UC Riverside with a B.S. in Computer Science. Currently, I'm enrolled in the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive program, with an end date of June 12, 2023. Check out my bio page for a bit more about me.

My interests in the tech sphere are drones/robotics and their simulation, data science, web and app development, and FDM 3D printing. I have been coding since 2016, when I recieved a Rasperry Pi for Christmas from my housemate/best friend Momo. I have listed some coursework on the Portfolio page, and left some spaces for the projects I will complete during the Immersive.

I am looking for entry-level roles or internships in the following areas: Fullstack, QA, Web Development, App Development. My resume is available on my bio page. Feel free to contact me via the methods listed on the Contact page.
