About Me

I am currently searching for my place in the software engineering world. I left my insurance accounting job in late 2018 to pursue my dream of earning a computer science degree. I was accepted to UCR's Bourns College of Engineering, and graduated 4 years later in June of 2022. During that time, I worked with a wide variety of tools and technologies, and absorbed many different software-related skill sets. I am comfortable programming in C, C++, python, JavaScript, and Java. I have created websites, apps, games, and devices, and I thoroughly enjoyed every step along the way.

College was a broad education, and so I decided to to deepen my better understanding for the hiring process by enrolling in the General Assembly SEI bootcamp. This program consisted of over 400 hours of class time, as well as homework and projects. My instructors taught me and my cohort a well-chosen set of skills, and I'm eager to put them to good use at a company whose mission resonates with me.

I pride myself on being someone who learns every day. I love mastering new skills, and I love to apply new knowledge. I particularly enjoy ChatGPT, and use it daily to understand new topics and formulate better questions. I have also completed many tech-related courses on Udemy and Coursera, and plan to continue doing so.

I have strong problem-solving and analytical skills, and am well practiced at breaking down larger problems into more manageable components. Check out my BlogFolio here for examples.

I also pride myself on being an excellent teammate. I know when to lead and when to follow, when to speak up and when to bite my tongue. I am affable, humorous, respectful, and flexible. I know when to ask for more work, and when to declare that I may be in over my head. I don't hide my mistakes, but rather use them as learning opportunities. I am eager to work alongside more experienced programmers and tech workers, and look forward to receiving a chance to do so.

In my spare time, I like to spend time with my girlfriend, family, and friends. Relax with my grumpy black cat, and hike with my Husky/Golden mix. I build, fly, crash, and repair drones.
